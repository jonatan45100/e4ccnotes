Rapport

 5 min

Ask a question (Ice breaker) show students how to properly answer the asked question. Once the question is answered correct mistakes. Make everyone to participate.

Homework Check

 5 min

4 simple steps, we must show pathwright, congratulate tops, get commitment from bottoms, ask for sentences learned in the natural method and show substitution. Stress the importance of doing homework. Circle of pressure and correct mistakes right after they provided their example.

Mental Trainig

 2 min

Positive affirmations, it is not about screaming, yelling nor doing crazy things however, physical activities might be involved just to make the class interactive.

Grammar

 12 - 15 min

Follow the instructions shown on the presentation, break down info into simple phrases so trainees can understand. Make them participate and provide examples of how they can use what you are teaching. Provide an example and ask students to give an example too.

Attendance

 2 min

We encourage trainees to have a perfect attendance rate. Say trainee’s name a couple of times and if they do no answer mark as “not present”

Speaking Activity

 10 min

Demonstration, Partner up, Class share, Questions (DPCQ). Demonstration: We show them what we will do, we provide examples and a quick explanation of the topic. Partner up: We send trainees to breakout rooms so they can practice. While they are in breakout rooms we audit and make sure they are practicing. Class share: Pick the trainees you will evaluate and tell the others to prepare questions for participant. Questions: Make everyone ask questions about what the participant just said. Feedback time: provide feedback based on mistakes made during presentation. Once feedback is given give one more chance and 10 more seconds to retell the story. Evaluate at least 2 students.

Assign EXPOS on Monday

 2 min

On Mondays we do not have expositions, we assign exposition. We provide instructions on how to do a proper presentation.

How to do EXPOS

 10 min

Pick a trainee and give him 30 seconds to give their presentation, ask everyone else to prepare questions about EXPO.

Automatic Fluency

 40 min

Ask a trainee to read the instructions and provide an example, once example has been provided send trainees to breakout rooms and audit by going from room to room making sure they are practicing (2 min max). MT once they are back, pick a trainee and ask everyone to prepare questions for participant. 30 seconds to participate and one the questions are asked provide feedback. 5 Participants evaluated.
